About us

Digital Walking Group is an online community space for local residents.

All are welcome, but we have a special focus on residents aged 60+.

Discover what is going on in your local area and share your experiences.

Code of Conduct

  • Be kind and welcoming to others
  • Provide a safe space regardless of gender identity, age, sexual orientation, disability race, ethnicity, religion, or other characteristics
  • Treat everyone fairly and with respect
  • Do not offer advice if it has not been asked for
  • Do not post personal information or advertisements in group spaces
  • Embrace best practice of online safety and safeguarding
  • Strive to have a positive impact on the community

About the team

Digital Walking Group is a team project presented by Vicky Barnett, Mobolaji Ogunyemi, Polina Schadauer-Povarich, and Emma Smart, for the TechUP / Durham University Skills Bootcamp in Software Development 2022.

Technologies we have used are: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Django, Git and GitHub.
